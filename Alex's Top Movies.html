My Favorite Movies

The Three Best Movies of All Time

Up

Up is an animated film about an old man's search for meaning in later life. After losing his wife he's explored the world with, he becomes disgruntled and fed up with the world. In order to escape, he creates a balloon floatation device for his home, that uplifts his home and ultimately his life. A dog obsessed with squirrels enters his life, to his chagrin, and he eventually makes a new friend.

The Matrix

The Matrix is in a class of its own, being one of the first thrillers that takes the protagonist, Neo, into a dream like world. Escaping his office job, he enters the Matrix where he is able to dodge bullets, and eventually saves the world. The source code is something that still resonates today, with lines scrolling over and over. He meets Morpheous and others in the Matrix and has a love affair with Trinity.

Swingers

Swingers is a comdey about a friendship. A man goes through a breakup and he's not able to get his life back together. His friend, played by Vince Vaughn, picks him up with a trip to Vegas. Vegas Baby, Vegas. After struggling try to get back with his ex, he eventually finds love again and just as his ex comes calling about the relationship, "the rub," he's finally moved on.
